Parkway Drive

Parkway Drive is an Australian metalcore band formed in 2003 in Byron Bay. Known for their powerful blend of melodic riffs, crushing breakdowns, and emotionally charged lyrics, the band has become one of the leading names in modern heavy music.

This is their original website.

Members

  • Winston McCall – lead vocals (2003–present)
  • Jeff Ling – lead guitar, backing vocals (2003–present)
  • Luke Kilpatrick – rhythm guitar, backing vocals (2003–present)
  • Jia O’Connor – bass guitar (2006–present)
  • Ben Gordon – drums, percussion (2003–present)

Top favourite songs:

  1. 🎵 “Wild Eyes” (Atlas, 2012) – An anthemic track with soaring melodies and a powerful chorus that became a live-show staple.
  2. 🔥 “Vice Grip” (Ire, 2015) – A turning point in their sound, mixing metalcore aggression with arena-ready hooks.
  3. ⚡ “The Void” (Reverence, 2018) – Heavy, cinematic, and emotionally charged, reflecting the band’s mature, epic style.
